Nadi Astrology

Nadi Astrology is clasified into three types.These three nadi are Adi (Begin), Madhya (Middle) and Antya (End). These nadis represent and correspond to the three basic Qualities or Prakritis of the Vaat, Pita, and Kapha, respectively.

Adi (Begin)

Adinadi includes Ashwani, Adra, Punarvasu, Uttaraphalguni, Hasta, Jyestha, Moola, Shatabhisha and PurvaBhadrapada.

read more

Madhya (Middle)

Madhyanadi includes Bharani, Mrigshira, Pushya, Purvaphalguni, Chitra, Anuradha, Purvashada, Uttara Bhadrapada and Dhanishtha.

read more

Antya (End)

Antyanadi comprises Krittika, Rohini, Ashlesha, Magha, Swarti, Vishakha, Uttarashada, Shravan and Revati constellations.
